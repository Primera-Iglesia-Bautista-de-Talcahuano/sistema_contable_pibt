Dashboard design decisions

  • Green hero card for saldo — the most important number gets the most visual weight. Ingresos/egresos are nested within it on mobile.
  • Trend bars + category breakdown side by side on desktop; stacked on mobile
  • Recent movements as a compact list on mobile, a mini-table on desktop — same data, adapted layout
  • Period selector (month picker) — top-right, applies to all cards at once
  • Movement counts on KPI cards ("28 movimientos") adds context without clutter
  • "Ver todos →" quick link to the full movements page from recent list
