Design decisions

  • Type selector as visual cards — not a dropdown. Ingreso/Egreso are the most important choice and deserve prominence. Selecting one updates the category chips below.
  • Category as pill chips — faster than a dropdown for a small fixed list. Selected chip turns green.
  • Desktop: two-column layout — required fields left, optional fields right. Users can ignore the right column entirely for simple entries.
  • Mobile: single column, grouped sections — each section is a card with a header. Optional sections clearly labeled.
  • Save button in the top bar on both breakpoints — always reachable without scrolling.
  • Active field highlighted with green border (amount field shown focused).
