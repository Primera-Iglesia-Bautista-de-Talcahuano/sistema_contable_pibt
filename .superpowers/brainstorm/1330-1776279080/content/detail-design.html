Design decisions

  • Green hero card at the top — amount, type, category and date at a glance before scrolling.
  • PDF actions inside the hero card (desktop) — "Ver PDF" and "Regenerar" where the eye already lands.
  • Fields in a 2-column grid — compact, scannable, no wasted vertical space.
  • Audit log as a vertical timeline — cleaner than a table, shows sequence clearly.
  • Technical status as small badges in the right sidebar — visible but not prominent. Only admins care.
  • Edit + Anular always visible in the top bar — no scrolling needed to take action.
