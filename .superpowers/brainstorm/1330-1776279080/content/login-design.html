Design decisions

  • Desktop: split screen — left half is the green verse panel, right half is the form on white. The verse has equal visual weight to the login form.
  • Mobile: stacked — verse panel on top (compact), form below. Same content, natural scroll order.
  • Cross icon + church name above the verse gives identity without a logo image.
  • Subtle circles in background add depth to the solid green without distracting.
  • "Acceso restringido" note at the bottom clarifies this isn't a public signup.
  • "¿Olvidaste tu contraseña?" link placed inline next to the label (desktop) to save space.
