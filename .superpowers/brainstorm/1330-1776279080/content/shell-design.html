Design decisions in this shell

  • Sidebar always visible on desktop — no collapse needed, 220px gives enough room for labels
  • "Nuevo Movimiento" at the top of the sidebar — the most frequent action is one click away from anywhere
  • Mobile drawer — full height, slides over content with a semi-transparent overlay behind
  • User profile at bottom of sidebar with initials avatar, role label, and overflow menu (settings, logout)
  • Page header strip (desktop only) — shows page title, subtitle (e.g. current month), and contextual status
  • No top navigation bar on desktop — sidebar handles everything, more vertical space for content
