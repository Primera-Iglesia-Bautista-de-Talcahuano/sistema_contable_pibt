A — Grid of person cards

Each user is a card with avatar, name, email, role badge and active toggle. "Invitar usuario" is the last card with a dashed border. Works great on mobile too.

B — Horizontal list cards

Each user is a wide card: avatar + name + email on the left, role badge + toggle + edit button on the right. Spacious, readable, naturally adapts to mobile. Most similar to a contacts app.
